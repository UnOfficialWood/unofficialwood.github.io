My Recent Projects

Battleship Project!

My first semester at OSU I was part of an engineering team for Fundamentals of Engineering course. For our semester project we were tasked with developing a battleship like video game in MATLAB with advertisement material and a full project write up. I was personally tasked with writing the MATLAB code for the battleship video game. I utilized the display graphics library and developed all the underlying mechanics and AI for the game. Testing and developing the AI algorithm for a player to play against helped me expand my skillset and learn every possible strategy for battleship.

Link to Github Repository for Battle Ship

Transportation Project!

My second semester at OSU I was part of an engineering team for Fundamentals of Engineering II course. The entirety of the semester was devoted towards creating and prototyping and creating a new mode of transportation around OSU’s campus and Columbus, Ohio. Due to the increase in traffic we decided to develop a monorail system that can move a lot of people safely and avoid vehicle traffic. We then expanded on the idea to add solar panels and other clean energy technologies along the routes and at each stop.

Link to Github Repository for Transportation Project

Particles Project!

Over the course of spring semester of 2020, I wanted to expand my web development knowledge. I decided to develop from scratch a backend, frontend, and routing for a custom domain and webservices. I developed a NodeJS backend and secure routing tables for other web services. I also developed a demo website that I served though the NodeJS server. This project allowed me to experiment with web technologies that I had not previously been exposed to and added something to demonstrate for job interviews. I enjoyed developing it and will continue to add to it.
